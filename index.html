bird-species-recognition: Automated Species Identification

High-accuracy classification for wildlife monitoring and chatbot integration.

Executive Summary: This project utilizes a ResNet + SPP layer-based module with EfficientNetB0 to achieve 95.8% accuracy in categorizing bird species from images, addressing critical identification needs.

Problem Statement:

Critical need for accurate identification of bird species in applications like wildlife monitoring and chatbot systems, overcoming challenges of structural similarities and class imbalances.

Solution & Approach:

Developed a deep learning model using Jupyter Notebook, EfficientNetB0, ResNet, and SPP layer, trained on a dataset of 525 distinct bird species for precise classification.

Technology Stack:

Jupyter Notebook, EfficientNetB0, ImageDataGenerator, ResNet, SPP layer, Adam optimizer.

Results & Impact:

Achieved 95.8% accuracy in bird species classification, significantly reducing manual effort in monitoring and enabling automated insights for improved data-driven decisions and operational efficiency.
